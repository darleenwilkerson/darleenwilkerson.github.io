Darleen Wilkerson

We need a tag line or something here

Whether on canvas or on cloth making art for me is fun. From childhood I was preoccupied with color and texture. I was drawn to bright colors, glitter and shinny accents in those early years and still get a lot of satisfaction from creating items which come from this child-like place within myself.

On this website you can view groups of styles and techniques which I use in my art work. My mixed media paintings reflect this long love affair with color, texture and images from my creative child spirit – yet – also influenced from my many years living and working around the world in textile product development.

Aside from the mixed media I show a calmer and more earthy side with a group of batiks. I have been making batiks since I was in college in the 70’s. Through my art career I have made batik stuffed sculpture, clothing and wall art. I have had luck I selling batiks for many years and always enjoy making them and are currently showing them throughout the area of California where I live.
